Recommended actions

  1. Strengthen card-not-present monitoringPrioritize online shopping, especially overnight.
  2. Review high-value transactionsApply additional checks to unusually large transactions, especially $250 and above.
  3. Use combined risk rulesBlend amount deviation, category, merchant history and customer baseline.
  4. Apply volume safeguardsRequire minimum sample sizes before escalating segments.
  5. Treat the findings as initial risk indicatorsBecause the analysis covers only one month, use these patterns to identify transactions for additional verification—not to automatically block them.
